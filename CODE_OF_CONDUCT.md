# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive
- Welcome newcomers and help them learn
- Accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what's best for the community

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information
- Other unprofessional conduct

## Enforcement

Report violations to conduct@rate-sync.dev. All reports will be reviewed promptly and kept confidential.

Consequences may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity.
